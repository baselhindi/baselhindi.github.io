My name is Basel Hindi. I am a Senior Software Engineer in AI/ML at Nike, where I work on agentic and generative AI systems used across the enterprise, and contribute to the architecture of production AI platforms.

### Areas of Interest

- Agentic AI systems  
- LLM evaluation and guardrails  
- Multimodal AI  
- Scalable AI infrastructure  

### Background

Before working on generative AI, I developed autonomy and perception software for autonomous vehicles, including sensor fusion and LiDAR-based decision systems.

I hold a Master of Science in Computer Science from Columbia University, where I was an NSF DREAM Research Fellow conducting research in computer vision and multimodal accessibility systems for blind/ low-vision people. Specifically, I worked on human-computer interaction with Professor [Brian A. Smith](http://www.cs.columbia.edu/~brian/index.html) in the [Computer-Enabled Abilities Laboratory (CEAL)](https://ceal.cs.columbia.edu).
